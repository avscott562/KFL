In Fantasy Football, we choose from real-life football players, sort them into a lineup, then face off against another friend’s team. We watch real-life football games to see how our players gained (or lost) points. Then, whoever’s team tallied the most points wins.

How points are calculated:

Defense:

    Each tackle = 1 point
    Sack = 2 points
    Interception = 3 points
    Touchdown by defense = 6 points
    Safety = 2 points

Offense:

    Each touchdown = 6 pts
    Each catch = 1 pt
    Each rush = 1 pt
    Every 10 yards = 1 pt
    Over 100 yards = 5 pts
    QB fumble or interception = -1 pt

Special Teams:

    Extra Point = 1pt
    Field Goal = 2pts (under 40yds)
    Field Goal = 3pts (over 40yds)
