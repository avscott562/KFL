How to Play

In FF, we choose from real-life football players, sort them into a lineup, then face off against another friend’s team. We watch real-life football games to see how our players gained (or lost) points. Then, whoever’s team tallied the most points wins.

How points are calculated:

Defense:

    Each tackle = 1 point
    Sack = 2 points
    Interception = 3 points
    Touchdown by defense = 6 points
    Safety = 2 points
